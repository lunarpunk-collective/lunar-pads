Lunar Pads

Eco-friendly menstrual products for the modern lunarpunk! Say goodbye to disposable pads and tampons that contribute to waste and pollution, and say hello to reusable cloth pads that are gentle on your body and the environment. These cloth pads are not only made from upcycled materials sourced from thrift stores, but they are also customizable with your own unique lunarpunk art through screen printing.

Instructions for Upcycling from Thrift Stores:

  1. Gather Materials: Visit thrift stores or second-hand shops to find old t-shirts made from soft and absorbent materials like cotton or bamboo. Look for shirts in good condition, without stains or holes, as they will serve as the base material for your cloth pads.
  2. Prepare Shirts: Wash and sanitize the shirts thoroughly before cutting them into rectangular or oval shapes to create the absorbent layers of the cloth pads. You can use a pattern or template to ensure consistent sizing and shape.
  3. Add Layers: Layer multiple cut shirts together to create the desired level of absorbency. You can use different colored shirts to create a unique and artistic look for your cloth pads.
  4. Sew: Use a sewing machine or hand-sew around the edges of the layered shirts to secure them together and create the cloth pad. You can add a waterproof layer made from upcycled materials like old shower curtains or use a water-resistant fabric to prevent leaks.
  5. Screen Print Lunarpunk Art: Once the cloth pads are sewn, you can screen print your own lunarpunk art or designs onto them using eco-friendly and non-toxic fabric paint. Use a screen printing kit or find a local print shop that offers screen printing services.
  6. Finish: Allow the fabric paint to dry completely, following the manufacturer's instructions. Once dry, your eco-friendly cloth pads with your unique lunarpunk art are ready to use!

By upcycling materials from thrift stores and incorporating screen printed lunarpunk art, you can create unique and eco-friendly menstrual products that align with the principles of sustainability and creativity embraced by the lunarpunk movement.

Here are general cleaning instructions for reusable cloth pads:

  1. Rinse: After each use, rinse the cloth pad with cold water to remove excess blood. Avoid using hot water as it can set stains.
  2. Soak: If desired, you can soak the cloth pad in cold water for a few hours or overnight to help remove stains and odors. You can add a gentle, eco-friendly detergent or stain remover to the water if needed.
  3. Wash: After soaking, wash the cloth pad in the washing machine with like-colored laundry using a gentle cycle and cold water. Avoid using bleach, fabric softeners, or harsh detergents as they can damage the fabric and impact absorbency.
  4. Dry: Hang or lay flat to dry in a well-ventilated area. Avoid using high heat or direct sunlight for drying as it can shrink or fade the fabric.
  5. Store: Once dry, fold or roll the cloth pad and store it in a clean and dry place until its next use.
  6. Screen Printed Art Care: If you have screen printed lunarpunk art on your cloth pad, follow the care instructions provided by the fabric paint manufacturer to ensure the longevity of the design. In general, avoid harsh rubbing or scrubbing on the printed area during cleaning.

It's important to note that different cloth pad materials and brands may have specific care instructions, so be sure to check the manufacturer's recommendations for the best results. Proper care and cleaning of reusable cloth pads will help maintain their performance, longevity, and hygiene, making them a sustainable and eco-friendly option for menstrual care.
